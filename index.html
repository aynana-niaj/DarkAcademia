A guide to dark Academia

Dark academia is a social media aesthetic and subculture centred on higher education, literature, poetry, arts, and classic Greek and Gothic architecture.

HISTORY

Donna Tartt's novel The Secret History, published in 1992 and which tells the story of a murder that takes place within a group of classics students at an elite New England college, has been credited as being the inspiration for the dark academia literary genre.


LIFESTYLE

Autumnal forests and occasional images of seaside cliffs. Idolised university life with a mysterious undertone adds to the lifestyle of its proponents. As the subculture focuses around academic institutions, lots of the activities involve academic hobbies.
